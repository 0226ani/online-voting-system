1.President:

The College President is the Chief Executive and Administrative Officer for the Board of Trustees. He/She will execute directly or by delegation, all executive and administrative duties in connection with the operation of the College.

2.Vice-President:

The Vice President for Student affairs supervises the five major units within the Division of Student affairs and is supervised by the President of the College.

3.Secretary:

The Secretary is responsilble for leading the Students Union. They are the external face of the Student Union and work with organisations and deal with press and media requests. They part take in negotiations between the School and SU.
